analyzing Airbnb Paris listings

I analyzed 45k rows of Airbnb listings in Paris using clustering and NLP. First, I performed EDA to get to know about the data. Then I used clustering (K-means, mean shift) to see different host groups. I analyzed listing descriptions using NLP (CountVectorizer, tf- idf, bi-grams, LSA), and random forest models to find keywords and help hosts to boost sales.
